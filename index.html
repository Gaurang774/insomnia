Product & Technology

Core Technology: 5-8 Hz Brainwave Entrainment

The core technological principle underpinning our sleep aid device is the application of sound frequencies within the 5-8 Hz range to facilitate and expedite the process of falling asleep. This approach leverages brainwave entrainment, where external stimuli can influence the brain's electrical activity.

The 5-8 Hz range corresponds to the theta brainwave state, typically associated with light sleep, meditation, and the transitional phase between wakefulness and sleep.

Technical Implementation

  • Small speaker or transducer strategically placed within eyemask
  • Bone conduction technology for direct sound delivery
  • Microcontroller with DAC for 5-8 Hz signal generation

Brainwave States

Beta (Awake) 14-30 Hz
Alpha (Relaxed) 8-13 Hz
Theta (Sleep) 5-8 Hz
Delta (Deep Sleep) 0.5-4 Hz

Technical Implementation

Sound Generation

Microcontroller programmed to produce precise 5-8 Hz sine wave, converted via DAC and amplified for optimal delivery.

Bone Conduction

Transducers transmit sound vibrations directly through skull bones to inner ear, ensuring comfort and effectiveness.

Power Management

Rechargeable battery with auto-cutoff feature after predetermined duration (5-30 minutes), optimized for power efficiency.
